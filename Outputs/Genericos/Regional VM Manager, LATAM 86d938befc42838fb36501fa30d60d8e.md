JD: Position Summary
We are looking for a highly creative, organized, and execution-focused Regional Visual Merchandising Manager to lead Visual Merchandising for Fenty across Latin America.

This role is responsible for translating global brand vision into impactful, locally relevant retail execution across a diverse regional footprint, primarily within specialty beauty retailers and department stores. The scope covers retail design, permanent fixtures, seasonal updates, new product launches, new country launches, planograms, and in-store visual standards, ensuring a strong and consistent brand expression across markets while adapting to local business needs and retail formats.

Reporting to: Senior Director, LATAM
Location: Mexico City, MEXICO

Key Responsibilities
- Lead VM strategy and execution for Fenty across LATAM, ensuring alignment with global brand standards and local market needs.
- Translate global guidelines into locally relevant solutions across countries, retail formats, and route-to-market models.
- Manage development, adaptation, and implementation of permanent fixtures (gondolas, counters, merchandising units).
- Oversee seasonal updates and graphic refreshes for core merchandising and launch collateral.
- Adapt global planograms and create market-specific planograms with focus on productivity, shopper flow, and clarity.
- Adapt and implement merchandising guidelines for new product launches, seasonal animations, and new country launches.
- Develop local POS materials from global assets, aligned with market specifications.
- Work directly with retailers and distributors to ensure strong execution of VM standards across all POS.
- Manage agencies, suppliers, freelancers, and production partners from briefing through delivery and installation.
- Own the VM budget for the region, managing OPEX and CAPEX.
- Track effectiveness of VM initiatives with focus on execution quality, productivity, and ROI.
- Conduct regular field visits to review implementation, maintenance, and visual consistency.

Candidate Profile
- Minimum 10 years of experience in Visual Merchandising, retail design, or related roles, ideally within beauty or adjacent premium consumer industries.
- Experience across Sephora, department store counters, specialty beauty retail, and travel retail is highly valued.
- Experience managing VM across multiple countries and working with distributors, retailers, and external vendors strongly preferred.
- Strong technical understanding of VM production and implementation.
- Excellent project management skills, multiple stakeholders, timelines, and priorities in parallel.
- Strong financial acumen, proven ability to manage budgets (CAPEX/OPEX).
- Portuguese and English required. Spanish is a strong plus.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office required. Adobe Creative Suite, InDesign, SketchUp, AutoCAD a plus.
- Open to travel across the region.
